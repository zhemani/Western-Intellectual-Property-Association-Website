About WIPA

Western University's Intellectual Property Association

The Western Intellectual Property Association is a student club that helps law students interested in intellectual property connect with lawyers who practice in this area of law. Events include career panel discussions, guest speaker events, and firm tours.

The club helps get students interested in intellectual property early in their legal education so they can make an informed decision about whether they are well-suited to this unique and fascinating area of practice. WIPA holds numerous events throughout the school year including guest lecturers, resume clinics, and firm tours. We also offer insights on other ways to stay connected within the IP Community which inlude the Fox Moot and the IPIT Stream.
